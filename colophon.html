What is all this?

Each person who views this site will view their own virtual cross stitch world. There are many possible colors and shapes, rivers and tributaries, growths and obstructions.

This artwork can create as many as 4 Billion possible worlds, created for each viewer using their unique device settings.

Inspired by Kafka's parable "Before the Law", the door has opened up a world that was made only for you. But unlike in Kafka's parable, this world is accessible to you. Enjoy!
